RENOVATION

Brooklane Family Dental

This was a remodel of an existing 2,250 square foot building in Hueytown, AL. The building was previously occupied by an alarm monitoring company, and the remodel began with a total demolition of the interior.

The program included 5 operatories, an office including a private restroom, patient restroom, employee restroom, break room, a lab, and a reception / waiting area.

I was responsible for the design, beginning with taking field measuremetns to create an existing plan of the building and ending with the production of the construction documents, which included the floor plan, reflected ceiling plan, electrical plan, the plumbing plan, and interior elevations.
