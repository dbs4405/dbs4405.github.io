Glen Shelly Auto Brokers

Glen Shelly Auto Brokers is an auto brokerage and dealer in Denver owned by two young airlines pilots. The owners desired a website that would differentiate themselves and their business from what is typical in the automobile business. In particular, the owners wanted to emphasize their love of the outdoors and outdoor activities as well as their enthusiast background.

From a technical standpoint, the owners requested a site that could be updated frequently and easily. To accomplish this, I chose to utilize Statamic, a flat-file CMS.

I designed and constructed the site, and while the design continues to evolve and features are added, I am updating and maintaining the site. Technologies used include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and Statamic flat-file CMS.
