Glen Shelly Auto Brokers

Glen Shelly Auto Brokers is an auto brokerage and dealer in Denver owned by two young airlines pilots. The owners desired a website that would differentiate them from their competitors and would emphasize both their love of outdoor activities as well as their enthusiast background.

I designed and constructed the site, and while the design continues to evolve and features are added, I am updating and maintaining the site. Technologies used include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and Statamic flat-file CMS.
