DESIGN

Proposed House Addition and Renovation

This is a proposed master suite addition and kitchen renovation of a 1914 house. The 405 sf master suite addtion to the second floor where the program includes a bedroom, two closets, and a bathroom with enclosed toilet. On the first floor the existing kitchen is renovated with increased square footage gained by shifting an interior wall as well as demolition of an existing pantry and an existing, unused stair case. A new deck, accessed from the kitchen via French doors, provides outdoor space, and new French doors replace a window in the existing family room provide a second connection to the deck.
