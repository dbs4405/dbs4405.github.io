DESIGN

Proposed House Addition and Renovation

This is a proposed master suite addition and kitchen renovation of a 1914 house. The 405 sf second-floor master suite addition sits atop the existing family room and is designed to seamlessly blend with the existing house.

The proposed renovation of the existing first-floor kitchen provides increased square footage by shifting an interior wall as well as demolition of an existing pantry and unused stair case. A new deck, accessed from the kitchen via French doors, provides outdoor space, and new French doors replacing a window in the existing family room provide a second connection to the deck.
