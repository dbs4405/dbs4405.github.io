CONCEPTUAL DESIGN | PROPOSED SITE WORK

Proposed design for a dental practice where the site is surrounded by a six-foot chain link fence. The design aims to provide an alternative fencing option that is cost-effective, functional, and attractive by utilizing standard pressure-treated lumber, off-the-shelf hardware, and the existing vertical fence posts. The existing vertical fence posts provide structural support and prevent the need to disrupt the concrete slab into which the vertical posts are anchored. Use of varying widths of lumber provides visual interest, and spacing of the lumber provides a measure of transparency. A custom-designed planter is incorporated into each fence panel.
